About me

Hey there! I'm Carolyn. I live in Minneapolis with my 12 year old Shih-Tzu Teo (like Leo with a "T"). I have lived in 4 different states, but somehow always end back in Minnesota. I assume that the snow will drive me away again. I have owned two businesses, both a swimsuit company in Las Vegas, NV (our swimsuits were uniforms at Rehab in Las Vegas for two years in a row), and a medical marijuana edible company in Colorado Springs, CO. I have intermittedly supplimented my income with serving and bartending. Unfortunately the industry has taken a turn and my fallback doesn't feel like a fail safe anymore.

I love to go on hikes and try out different outdoor activities. Archery and snowboarding are two of my favorites. I love any body of water and am always down for a swim. I will never turn down a live music concert, and can have a good time ever if the music isn't my favorite type.
